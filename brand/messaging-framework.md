# Messaging Framework

## Purpose

This document defines the key messages the company should repeat across the site, product pages, captions, DMs, WhatsApp, and customer service.

Messaging must be consistent.
Different words can be used, but the same strategic ideas should keep showing up.

---

## Core Message Pillars

### Pillar 1: Factory Direct
We are closer to the source.

What this means:
- better access
- better quality control
- better pricing logic
- less middleman noise

Key phrases:
- direct from source
- factory-direct
- closer to the source
- no middleman markup

---

### Pillar 2: Premium Quality
The product must feel premium in language and visuals.

What this means:
- softness
- movement
- fullness
- polished finish
- natural look

Key phrases:
- premium raw Vietnamese hair
- soft movement
- real fullness
- clean finish
- quality you can feel

---

### Pillar 3: Better Value
We are not “cheap.”
We are stronger value.

What this means:
- pricing should feel sensible relative to quality
- direct sourcing justifies the value story
- customer feels smart, not bargain-hungry

Key phrases:
- better quality, better value
- premium without the usual markup
- better sourcing, better pricing
- quality without nonsense pricing

---

### Pillar 4: Ghana-Local Ease
The company should feel accessible and real.

What this means:
- easy ordering
- local support
- smooth communication
- practical guidance

Key phrases:
- Ghana-local support
- easy ordering
- message us directly
- we’ll guide you properly

---

### Pillar 5: Guidance and Clarity
We help customers choose.

What this means:
- lower confusion
- easier decisions
- more trust
- more conversion

Key phrases:
- need help choosing?
- we’ll guide you
- build your package your way
- let us recommend the best fit

## Messaging by Funnel Stage

### Awareness Stage
Goal:
grab attention and position the brand

Messages:
- luxury hair direct from source
- factory-direct quality
- the direct plug for premium raw Vietnamese hair
- quality without the usual markup

---

### Consideration Stage
Goal:
build trust and reduce uncertainty

Messages:
- how many bundles you need
- closure vs frontal
- what makes the quality different
- why direct sourcing matters
- premium quality with local support

---

### Conversion Stage
Goal:
close the sale

Messages:
- our most wanted bundle sets
- build your package your way
- need help choosing?
- shop bundles, wigs, or custom packages
- less guesswork, more finish

---

### Retention Stage
Goal:
increase repeat purchase and trust

Messages:
- care guidance
- your next set
- custom reorder help
- ready for a different look?
- maintain the quality, keep the finish

## Homepage Message Hierarchy

When someone lands on the homepage, they should understand:

1. what we sell
2. why it is different
3. why the value is stronger
4. how to buy
5. what to do if they are unsure

Suggested homepage order:
- main value proposition
- sourcing/value explanation
- bundles/wigs/custom options
- trust/proof
- guidance
- CTA

---

## Product Page Message Hierarchy

Each product page should communicate:

1. what it is
2. why it is good
3. who it is for
4. how much of it to buy
5. what makes it a strong value
6. what to do next

---

## Repetition Rules

Some ideas should repeat often:
- direct from source
- premium raw Vietnamese hair
- better quality, better pricing logic
- women in Ghana who know the difference
- build your package your way
- need help choosing?

Repetition is good when it is controlled.

---

## Things We Should Not Say

Avoid:
- fake scientific quality claims
- “best hair in Africa” nonsense
- cheap/cheapest messaging
- overuse of urgency
- desperate “buy now” spam
- anything that weakens the premium image

---

## Final Rule

Every message should support at least one of these:
- trust
- value
- quality
- ease
- conversion

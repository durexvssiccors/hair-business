# Customer Personas

## Overview

This company serves women in Ghana who care about looking polished, buying quality, and avoiding overpriced nonsense.

There are four primary personas.

---

## Persona 1: The Soft-Life Striver

### Summary
The core volume customer.
She wants to look expensive and put-together, even if she is still climbing financially.

### Age Range
21–32

### Profile
- image-conscious
- active on Instagram and WhatsApp
- beauty spending is a priority
- wants softness, fullness, and a finished look
- compares prices but still cares deeply about quality

### Motivations
- wants to look elevated
- wants to avoid fake "raw hair"
- wants quality without being exploited on price
- wants confidence at events, church, birthdays, brunch, dates, and photos

### Pain Points
- too many pages look untrustworthy
- hard to know what is really premium
- confusion around bundles, closures, frontals, and wigs
- fear of paying too much for weak quality

### Best Product Fit
- core bundle packages
- 3 bundles + closure
- medium to long lengths
- event-ready or everyday glam looks

### Best Messaging
- "Luxury hair. Better value."
- "Factory-direct quality without the markup."
- "For girls who know the difference."

### Best Channels
- Instagram
- WhatsApp
- referrals from beauty girls
- reposted install content

### Buying Style
Needs confidence and proof.
Usually converts after seeing clear quality and feeling guided.

---

## Persona 2: The Rich Polished Buyer

### Summary
The margin customer.
She has real spending power and wants convenience, polish, and trust.

### Age Range
26–40

### Profile
- executive, entrepreneur, partner, or upper-tier social buyer
- wants premium service and premium results
- cares less about bargain hunting, more about smooth quality access
- interested in wigs, premium lengths, and custom orders

### Motivations
- wants ease
- wants the final look to be right
- wants a polished, discreet buying process
- values quality and presentation

### Pain Points
- tacky pages turn her off
- too much slang feels cheap
- uncertainty about product quality
- bad customer service kills trust instantly

### Best Product Fit
- elite offers
- premium wigs
- extra-long lengths
- custom build orders
- VIP recommendation flows

### Best Messaging
- "Premium quality, cleaner experience."
- "Luxury hair direct from source."
- "Build your package your way."

### Best Channels
- premium-feeling Instagram content
- direct recommendation
- stylist referral
- private WhatsApp consultation

### Buying Style
Converts when the process feels smooth, premium, and low-friction.

---

## Persona 3: The Beauty Operator

### Summary
The multiplier.
She is a stylist, salon-linked buyer, reseller, or beauty professional.

### Age Range
23–40

### Profile
- knows hair terminology
- buys with practical intent
- may buy for herself and for clients
- values consistency, repeatability, and flexibility

### Motivations
- wants a dependable source
- wants strong value on quality
- wants flexible ordering
- may eventually want quantity

### Pain Points
- vague product pages
- unclear stock
- inconsistent quality
- sellers who do not understand professional use cases

### Best Product Fit
- custom build
- multi-item orders
- closures and frontals
- mixed quantities
- repeat order flows

### Best Messaging
- "Direct sourcing. Cleaner pricing."
- "Build the exact package you need."
- "Made for women who know quality."

### Best Channels
- direct outreach
- WhatsApp
- stylist networks
- beauty referrals

### Buying Style
Wants clarity, practicality, and less fluff.

---

## Persona 4: The Occasion Buyer

### Summary
The campaign customer.
She buys when the moment matters.

### Age Range
21–38

### Profile
- buying for wedding
- buying for birthday
- buying for graduation
- buying for holidays
- buying for a trip or event

### Motivations
- wants the look to land
- wants guidance
- wants speed and certainty
- wants to feel beautiful and finished

### Pain Points
- fear of choosing wrong
- fear of product not matching the event vibe
- fear of delay
- fear of low quality in a high-stakes moment

### Best Product Fit
- occasion bundles
- wigs
- elite looks
- guided recommendation offers

### Best Messaging
- "Event-ready luxury."
- "A finished look without guesswork."
- "Need help choosing? We’ve got you."

### Best Channels
- seasonal campaigns
- wedding content
- birthday-focused content
- reels showing transformations

### Buying Style
Responds well to urgency, confidence, and visual proof.

## Final Rule

Every campaign, offer, product page, and caption should answer:
**Which persona is this for?**

If the answer is unclear, the asset is not ready.

# Offers

## Offer Philosophy

We do not just sell hair.
We sell clarity, quality, confidence, and better sourcing.

Our offer ladder is built to:
- increase conversion
- increase average order value
- reduce confusion
- serve different buyer types
- make custom buying easier

There are four core tiers.

---

## Tier 1: Entry Premium

### Purpose
Bring in first-time buyers and lower-friction customers.

### Who It Is For
- Soft-Life Striver
- first-time premium buyer
- buyers who want a cleaner starting point

### Offer Characteristics
- simpler choices
- lower total spend than larger packages
- easy to understand
- low confusion

### Typical Products
- single bundle
- two-bundle starter set
- basic closure add-on
- shorter lengths

### Messaging Angle
- premium without overwhelm
- easy entry into better quality
- starter luxury

### Sample Offer Names
- Soft Start Bundle
- Starter Luxury Set
- First Look Package

## Offer Rules

### Rule 1
Every offer must clearly match a persona.

### Rule 2
Every offer must have one primary CTA.

### Rule 3
Every offer must have a clear value explanation.

### Rule 4
No product bundle should exist without a reason.

### Rule 5
The customer should always understand:
- what is included
- who it is for
- why it is worth it
- what to do next

## Offer Naming Principles

Offer names should feel:
- premium
- clear
- feminine
- easy to remember
- not corny
- not too generic

Good:
- Signature Bundle
- Full Finish Set
- Build Your Package
- Soft Life Deluxe

Bad:
- Mega Hair Explosion Deal
- Super Saver Combo
- Queen Boss Luxury Slay Pack

---

## Offer Review Checklist

Before approving any offer, confirm:
- who is this for
- what problem does it solve
- why is this better than buying items separately
- what does it increase: conversion, AOV, margin, or flexibility
- what is the CTA
- what content will support it

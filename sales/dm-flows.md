# DM Flows

## Purpose

Instagram DMs are for:
- answering inquiries
- qualifying interest
- moving the customer toward a decision
- pushing to WhatsApp or checkout
- creating a smooth first impression

DMs should not become endless chatting with no direction.

---

## Flow 1: “How much?”

### Response
Hi love — which texture or item are you interested in?

We have:
- bundles
- closures
- frontals
- wigs
- custom packages

If you want, tell me what look you’re going for and I’ll guide you properly.

### Goal
Move from generic price question to product qualification.

---

## Flow 2: “Do you have body wave?”

### Response
Yes, we do.

What lengths are you looking for — and do you want bundles only, a wig, or a full package?

### Goal
Move quickly to specifics.

---

## Flow 3: “I need help choosing”

### Response
Absolutely.

Tell me:
- the look you want
- texture you like
- bundles or wig
- length range
- whether it’s for everyday wear or an event

I’ll recommend the best fit.

### Goal
Start mini consultation.

---

## Flow 4: Recommendation Given

### Response
Based on what you described, I’d recommend [PRODUCT].

It’s a strong fit because:
- [reason 1]
- [reason 2]
- [reason 3]

If you want, I can send you the next step on WhatsApp so it’s easier to finalize.

### Goal
Move to WhatsApp.

---

## Flow 5: “Send price list”

### Response
We can guide you more accurately if we know what you want, but yes — we can send options.

Are you looking for:
- bundles
- wigs
- closures/frontals
- a custom package

Once I know that, I’ll point you in the right direction.

### Goal
Avoid dumping a giant generic list too early.

---

## Flow 6: Premium Buyer Tone

### Response
Yes, we do.

If you tell me the exact look you want, I can recommend the best option and the cleanest combination for it.

### Goal
Feel polished, not noisy.

---

## Flow 7: Push to WhatsApp

### Response
I can help you finalize this properly on WhatsApp.

Send us a message there and we’ll guide you through the best option:
**[WhatsApp link or number]**

### Goal
Move serious buyers into the closer channel.

---

## Flow 8: “I’m not sure if I want bundles or a wig”

### Response
That depends on the kind of finish and convenience you want.

Bundles are great if you’re building a custom install.
A wig is great if you want a more ready-made premium option.

If you want, I can recommend which is better for your look.

### Goal
Reduce confusion and keep momentum.

---

## Flow 9: Follow-Up on Interested Lead

### Response
Hi love — just checking back in.

Did you still want help picking the best option? I can recommend the right texture and quantity based on the look you want.

### Goal
Restart the conversation cleanly.

---

## Flow 10: Hard Close

### Response
This would be a great choice for what you described.

Would you like to:
1. place the order
2. see a close-up video first
3. build a custom package

### Goal
Present clear next actions.

---

## DM Rules

### Rule 1
Never answer “how much?” with a messy paragraph.

### Rule 2
Always qualify first when possible.

### Rule 3
Push serious buyers to WhatsApp.

### Rule 4
Sound smooth and premium.

### Rule 5
Every DM should move the conversation forward.

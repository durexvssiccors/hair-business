# KPI Definitions

## Purpose

This document defines the key performance indicators for the business.

We track metrics to:
- understand what is working
- identify bottlenecks
- improve conversion
- improve margin
- make the company quantifiable and sellable later

---

## 1. Revenue Metrics

### Daily Revenue
Total revenue generated in one day.

Why it matters:
Shows short-term commercial activity.

---

### Weekly Revenue
Total revenue generated in one week.

Why it matters:
Helps track momentum and campaign performance.

---

### Monthly Revenue
Total revenue generated in one month.

Why it matters:
Core top-line business health metric.

---

### Average Order Value (AOV)
Total revenue divided by number of orders.

Formula:
`AOV = Revenue / Orders`

Why it matters:
Shows how much customers spend per order.

Improvement levers:
- bundles
- custom build
- upsells
- premium offers

---

## 2. Conversion Metrics

### Website Conversion Rate
Percentage of visitors who place an order.

Formula:
`Orders / Website Sessions`

Why it matters:
Shows whether the site is actually selling.

---

### Product Page Conversion Rate
Percentage of product page visitors who purchase that product.

Formula:
`Product Orders / Product Page Views`

Why it matters:
Shows which products actually convert.

---

### Add-to-Cart Rate
Percentage of site visitors who add an item to cart.

Formula:
`Add to Cart Events / Website Sessions`

Why it matters:
Shows product interest before checkout.

---

### Checkout Start Rate
Percentage of sessions that begin checkout.

Formula:
`Begin Checkout / Website Sessions`

Why it matters:
Shows movement from interest to serious buying behavior.

---

### Cart Abandonment Rate
Percentage of carts not converted into completed purchases.

Formula:
`1 - (Purchases / Add to Cart Events)`

Why it matters:
Shows friction in checkout or hesitation.

---

## 3. Inquiry-to-Sale Metrics

### DM Inquiry Count
Number of Instagram DMs with purchase intent.

Why it matters:
Measures social interest.

---

### WhatsApp Inquiry Count
Number of WhatsApp conversations with purchase intent.

Why it matters:
Measures direct buying activity.

---

### DM-to-Sale Conversion Rate
Percentage of DM leads that convert to orders.

Formula:
`DM Orders / DM Inquiries`

Why it matters:
Shows whether Instagram leads are being closed well.

---

### WhatsApp-to-Sale Conversion Rate
Percentage of WhatsApp leads that convert to orders.

Formula:
`WhatsApp Orders / WhatsApp Inquiries`

Why it matters:
Shows the strength of the closer channel.

---

### Lead Response Time
Average time between inquiry and first response.

Why it matters:
Faster response usually increases conversion.

---

## 4. Product Metrics

### Top-Selling SKU
The SKU generating the most orders or revenue.

Why it matters:
Shows what the market wants most.

---

### Revenue by SKU
Total revenue generated per product.

Why it matters:
Helps prioritize products and marketing.

---

### Margin by SKU
Estimated gross margin for each product.

Formula:
`Sale Price - Cost Basis`

Why it matters:
Some products sell well but may not be strong businesses.

---

### Collection Performance
Revenue and conversion grouped by collection.

Examples:
- wigs
- body wave
- bone straight
- closures
- custom build

Why it matters:
Shows which product families deserve more focus.

---

## 5. Customer Metrics

### Repeat Purchase Rate
Percentage of customers who buy again.

Formula:
`Repeat Customers / Total Customers`

Why it matters:
Shows whether customers come back.

---

### Customer Lifetime Value Estimate (LTV)
Estimated revenue from a customer across repeat orders.

Why it matters:
Critical for long-term business value.

---

### Refund / Exchange Rate
Percentage of orders that result in a refund or exchange request.

Why it matters:
Shows quality, expectation, or service problems.

---

### Customer Satisfaction Notes
Qualitative review of recurring praise or complaints.

Why it matters:
Not every important signal is numeric.

---

## 6. Marketing Metrics

### Content-to-Inquiry Rate
How often content leads to DMs or WhatsApp inquiries.

Why it matters:
Shows which posts actually create buying intent.

---

### Traffic by Channel
Where visitors come from.

Examples:
- Instagram
- WhatsApp
- direct
- Google search
- referrals

Why it matters:
Shows where attention is turning into business.

---

### Campaign Conversion Rate
Orders or inquiries resulting from a specific campaign.

Why it matters:
Helps compare creative and offers.

---

### Best Performing Content Bucket
Which type of content drives the strongest outcomes.

Buckets:
- proof
- education
- offer
- identity
- social proof

Why it matters:
Guides what to create more of.

---

## 7. Operational Metrics

### Order Processing Time
Time between order placement and order confirmation/processing.

Why it matters:
Shows operational smoothness.

---

### Fulfillment Time
Time between order confirmation and delivery or dispatch.

Why it matters:
Critical for trust and event-based customers.

---

### Catalog Completion Rate
Percentage of imported assets that have been tagged and assigned structured metadata.

Why it matters:
Shows whether the product system is getting built properly.

---

### Product Publishing Rate
Number of completed product pages published over time.

Why it matters:
Tracks catalog buildout.

---

## 8. Strategic Metrics

### Offer Mix
Percentage of revenue by offer tier.

Tiers:
- Entry Premium
- Core Bundle
- Elite
- Custom

Why it matters:
Shows where the business is actually making money.

---

### Persona Mix
Estimate of which customer personas are generating the most revenue.

Why it matters:
Helps focus the business.

---

### Sales Channel Mix
Revenue split by:
- website
- WhatsApp
- Instagram DM
- custom build

Why it matters:
Shows what type of company you are actually becoming.

---

## KPI Priority Order

### Tier 1 KPIs
Track immediately:
- Daily Revenue
- Monthly Revenue
- AOV
- Website Conversion Rate
- DM-to-Sale Conversion Rate
- WhatsApp-to-Sale Conversion Rate
- Top-Selling SKU
- Repeat Purchase Rate

### Tier 2 KPIs
Track after launch:
- Margin by SKU
- Collection Performance
- Campaign Conversion Rate
- Fulfillment Time
- Cart Abandonment Rate

### Tier 3 KPIs
Track once systems mature:
- LTV
- Persona Mix
- Offer Mix
- Catalog Completion Rate
- Product Publishing Rate

---

## Final Rule

Do not track metrics just because they exist.
Track metrics that help make decisions.

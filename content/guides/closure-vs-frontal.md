# Closure vs Frontal

## What’s the Difference?

If you are building an install, one of the most common questions is:
**Should I choose a closure or a frontal?**

Both can help create a cleaner, more polished final look.
The better choice depends on the result you want.

---

## What Is a Closure?

A closure is a smaller lace piece used to help create a neat, natural-looking finish.

### A closure is good if you want:
- a clean result
- a more natural finish
- less maintenance
- a simpler option

For many customers, a closure is the easiest and most practical choice.

---

## What Is a Frontal?

A frontal is a larger lace piece that gives you more styling flexibility.

### A frontal is good if you want:
- more styling options
- a more dramatic final look
- more freedom in how the hair is worn
- a stronger glam finish

---

## Closure vs Frontal: Simple Comparison

### Choose a Closure if:
- you want easier upkeep
- you want a cleaner everyday option
- you prefer a more straightforward install path

### Choose a Frontal if:
- you want more styling range
- you want a more statement finish
- you want a more flexible glam option

---

## Which One Is Better?

Neither is universally “better.”
It depends on:
- your style
- your maintenance preference
- how dramatic you want the final look to be
- how much flexibility you want

---

## Who Usually Chooses a Closure?

Closures are often a strong choice for:
- everyday wear
- customers who want lower-maintenance options
- women who want a polished but practical finish

---

## Who Usually Chooses a Frontal?

Frontals are often a strong choice for:
- glam looks
- event looks
- customers who want more styling freedom
- buyers who want a more dramatic final effect

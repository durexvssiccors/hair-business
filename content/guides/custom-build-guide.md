# Build Your Own Package

## Create Your Package Your Way

This option is for customers who want more flexibility than a standard bundle set.

Choose exactly what you want:
- bundles
- closures
- frontals
- wigs
- mixed textures
- mixed lengths
- multiple quantities

You can build something simple or go all the way.

---

## Who This Is For

This option is perfect for:
- girls who already know what they want
- buyers who want mixed lengths
- women building a custom install
- premium shoppers who want more control
- stylists and beauty professionals
- customers buying multiple items at once

---

## What You Can Add

### Bundles
Choose your preferred texture and length.

Examples:
- Bone Straight
- Body Wave
- Deep Wave
- Curly

### Closures
Add a closure for a cleaner, more polished finish.

### Frontals
Choose a frontal for more styling flexibility and a more dramatic final look.

### Wigs
Add a wig if you want a full unit instead of a standard install.

### Multiple Items
You can combine categories in one package.

Example:
- 3 body wave bundles
- 1 straight closure
- 1 deep wave wig

---

## How It Works

### Step 1
Choose your texture.

### Step 2
Choose your length or lengths.

### Step 3
Choose how many bundles, closures, frontals, or wigs you want.

### Step 4
Submit your custom build request.

### Step 5
We confirm availability, pricing, and next steps.

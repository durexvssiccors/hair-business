# 📽️ Storyboard: Transformation (The Switch Up)

## 🎞️ Intent & Vibe
Showcase the incredible versatility of raw hair by featuring a dramatic "Silver Ombre" transformation. Highlight that raw hair can handle high-lift color without compromising integrity.

## 📸 Visual Production Prompt (AI-Generated)
**Subject:** A stunning West African model with dark skin that contrasts perfectly with a silver ombre look.
**Product:** 24-inch Raw Vietnamese hair transitioning from natural black roots to a shimmering silver ombre.
**Environment:** High-end urban art gallery or a minimalist loft.
**Lighting:** Dramatic, high-contrast lighting to emphasize the silver tones and the hair's sleekness.
**Camera:** 8k, portrait lens with shallow depth of field.
**Aesthetic:** High-fashion, avant-garde luxury.

# 📽️ Storyboard: The Flagship

## 🎞️ Intent & Vibe
Establish the brand as the "Direct Plug" for luxury. Focus on the physical quality of the hair — its movement and shine — to contrast with the stiff, synthetic-coated "processed" hair common in local markets.

## 📸 Visual Production Prompt (AI-Generated)
**Subject:** A striking West African woman with deep mahogany skin, sharp high cheekbones, and a direct confident gaze into the camera.
**Hair:** 24-inch bone straight raw Vietnamese hair, natural black — pin-straight strands with a mirror-like polish, moving naturally as if caught mid-swing. Individual strands catch the rim light beautifully.
**Outfit:** A structured black off-shoulder top — clean and minimal so nothing competes with the hair.
**Environment:** Charcoal grey seamless studio backdrop.
**Lighting:** Cinematic Rembrandt setup — strong key light from the upper left, warm golden rim light grazing the hair's edge to reveal individual strand detail and luminous depth.
**Composition:** Three-quarter portrait, medium close-up, slightly below eye level looking up at subject.
**Style:** Vogue Africa editorial fashion photography, photorealistic, ultra-detailed skin texture, visible hair cuticles, cinematic film-grade color grading, warm-to-neutral tones.
**Negative:** No text, no watermarks, no blurry backgrounds, no synthetic-looking plastic shine on hair.

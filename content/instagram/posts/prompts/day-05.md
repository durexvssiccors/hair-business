Hyper-focus on texture. Educate the viewer on what "Raw" actually looks like — no synthetic shine, naturally aligned cuticles, real definition. Make the viewer want to touch it.

## 📸 Visual Production Prompt (AI-Generated)
**Subject:** Extreme macro close-up of Raw Vietnamese Deep Wave hair strands arranged loosely against a neutral matte surface.
**Product:** Natural black Deep Wave hair — the "mountain and valley" wave pattern clearly three-dimensional. Individual strands show natural luster (not plastic or metallic sheen). Slight natural moisture visible on the cuticle. Richly defined, real-looking texture throughout.
**Surface:** Smooth charcoal grey matte surface — stone tile or dark textured linen to create tactile contrast with the hair.
**Lighting:** Dramatic raking side-light from the right — creates strong relief across the wave crests, casting soft shadows into the valleys to emphasize the 3D texture depth.

**Negative:** No text, no plastic-looking shine, no oily-looking hair, no artificial gloss or coated-looking strands.

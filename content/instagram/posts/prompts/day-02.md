# 📽️ Storyboard: The Density Math

## 🎞️ Intent & Vibe
Position the brand as an "Expert Advisor." Address a common pain point in Ghana (thin, flat installs) by educating the customer on density vs. length.

## ✍️ Social Caption
Here's the bundle math for a full, expensive install.

— 14–16 inches: 2 bundles
— 18–20 inches: 3 bundles
— 22–26 inches: 3–4 bundles
— 28 inches+: 4 bundles minimum

Thin ends and a flat finish happen when you undercount. Our Double Drawn bundles stay full from root to tip — so the math actually works in your favor.

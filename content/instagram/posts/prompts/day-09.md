## ✍️ Social Caption
Closure vs. Frontal — the real breakdown.

Closure (4x4, 5x5): Fixed parting space, lower maintenance, easier install. Best for defined part styles. Great everyday option.

Frontal (13x4, 13x6): Hairline-to-hairline coverage, free-parting anywhere, maximum styling versatility. More maintenance required but more freedom.

Both look premium when done right. The choice depends on how you live.

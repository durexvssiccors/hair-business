# Messaging Angle Bank

Use these angles across:
- Instagram posts
- captions
- ads
- product pages
- WhatsApp conversations

---

## Angle 1 — “This Is What Quality Looks Like”

Focus:
- texture
- movement
- fullness

Examples:
- “You can see the difference immediately”
- “This is not average hair”
- “Look at how it moves”

---

## Angle 2 — “Stop Overpaying for Less”

Focus:
- price-to-quality advantage

Examples:
- “Same category, different pricing logic”
- “What you’ve been overpaying for”
- “Better hair, smarter pricing”

---

## Angle 3 — “Get the Look Right the First Time”

Focus:
- guidance
- bundle selection
- full installs

Examples:
- “3 bundles vs 4 bundles explained”
- “Why your install didn’t come out right”
- “What you actually need for fullness”

---

## Angle 4 — “Luxury Without Stress”

Focus:
- simplicity
- ease of buying

Examples:
- “No confusion, just options that make sense”
- “Choose your look without the headache”
- “We made this simple”

---

## Angle 5 — “Custom Means Control”

Focus:
- custom builder

Examples:
- “Build exactly what you want”
- “No fixed packages, your rules”
- “Bundles, closures, wigs — mix it your way”

---

## Angle 6 — “Real Results”

Focus:
- before/after
- installs
- real customers

Examples:
- “This is how it actually comes out”
- “Installed and styled”
- “No filters needed”

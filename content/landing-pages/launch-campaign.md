# Launch Campaign

## Phase 1 — Warm-Up (3–5 days)

Post:
- quality previews
- close-ups
- textures
- “something is coming”

Goal:
Build curiosity

---

## Phase 2 — Reveal

Post:
- brand intro
- what makes us different
- first offers

CTA:
Shop / WhatsApp

---

## Phase 3 — Proof

Post:
- installs
- transformations
- real results

Goal:
Build trust fast

---

## Phase 4 — Push

Post:
- bundles
- packages
- custom builder

CTA:
Buy / Build

---

## Phase 5 — Stabilize

Post:
- FAQs
- education
- repeat offers

Goal:
Consistency + conversion

# The Ghana Hair Guide: Stop Overpaying for Middlemen

If you’ve ever wondered why the same 24-inch bundle costs twice as much in one shop as it does in another, you aren't alone. The hair industry is notoriously opaque, especially in West Africa.

Today, we’re pulling back the curtain on the supply chain to show you why "Factory Direct" isn't just a buzzword—it's a massive saving for your wallet.

## The Traditional Supply Chain

Typically, hair goes through a long journey before it reaches you in Accra or Kumasi:
1. **The Donor:** Source in Vietnam.
2. **The Factory:** Processing and bundling.
3. **The Global Wholesaler:** Bulk buyers in Asia or Europe.
4. **The Local Importer:** The person bringing it into Ghana.
5. **The Retailer:** The boutique or IG shop you buy from.

Each one of those steps adds a markup. By the time it hits your head, you might be paying 300% more than the factory cost.

## The Direct Plug Advantage

Our goal isn't to be the "cheapest" in the market. Cheap hair usually means low quality. Our goal is to offer **Elite Quality at Fair Pricing**. Because we don't pay middleman markups, we can sell Raw Vietnamese hair for the same price others sell processed "Virgin" hair.

## What is Raw Vietnamese Hair?

Raw hair is exactly what it sounds like: hair in its most natural state. It has not been steam-treated to create artificial wave patterns, and it has certainly not been chemically processed or coated in silicone to hide damage.

Our Vietnamese hair is sourced directly from donors in Vietnam. It is selected for its strength, natural cuticle alignment, and longevity.

## The Problem with "Processed" Luxury

Most "high-end" hair sold in markets today is actually processed. To get those perfect, uniform waves, the hair is steamed. To get that ultra-shiny look, it's dipped in silicone. 

The problem? Once that silicone coating washes off, you are left with the underlying dry, damaged hair.

## Why Vietnamese Hair Wins in 2026

3. **Authentic Movement:** Raw hair has a "bounce" and movement that processed hair can never replicate. It looks like your hair, only better.

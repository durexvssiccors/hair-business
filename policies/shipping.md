# Shipping Policy

## Shipping

We aim to keep ordering simple, clear, and smooth.

Shipping and delivery details may vary based on:
- product type
- order size
- whether the order is standard or custom
- current stock status
- delivery location

If you need help before ordering, message us directly and we will guide you.

---

## Order Processing

Orders are reviewed and processed after confirmation.

Processing time may vary depending on:
- item availability
- custom build requests
- mixed-item orders
- order volume

We will confirm next steps once your order is being processed.

---

## Custom Orders

Custom Build orders may require additional confirmation because they can involve:
- mixed lengths
- mixed categories
- special quantities
- styling-specific combinations

If your order is custom, we may contact you to confirm final details before processing.

---

## Delivery Timing

Delivery timing depends on:
- your location
- the type of product ordered
- whether the order is standard or custom

If you are ordering for a birthday, wedding, trip, or other important event, we strongly recommend ordering early and messaging us directly before purchase.

---

## Shipping Updates

We aim to keep customers informed throughout the process.

If you need an update on your order, message us and we will help clarify the current status.

---

## Address Accuracy

Please make sure your order details and delivery information are correct.

We are not responsible for issues caused by incorrect customer-provided information.

---

## Delays

While we work to keep things smooth, delays can occasionally happen due to:
- order verification
- custom build complexity
- logistics issues
- high order volume

If there is a significant issue affecting your order, we will communicate clearly.

---

## Need Help?

If you have a delivery question or need help before placing an order, message us directly.

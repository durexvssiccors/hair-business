# Returns & Exchange Policy

## Returns and Exchanges

We care about customer trust and want the buying experience to feel clear and fair.

Because hair products are beauty-use items, return and exchange eligibility may depend on the condition of the product and the type of issue raised.

If you have a concern, contact us clearly and as early as possible.

---

## Contact Us First

If there is an issue with your order, please send:
- your order details
- a clear photo or video
- a short explanation of the concern

This helps us review the matter properly and guide you on the next step.

---

## Eligibility Considerations

Return or exchange review may depend on:
- whether the item has been used
- whether the product has been altered
- whether lace has been cut or customized
- the condition of the hair
- the nature of the issue

---

## Custom Orders

Custom Build orders and specially structured orders may have different handling because they are created around the customer’s specific request.

If you are ordering a custom package, make sure all details are confirmed properly before finalizing.

---

## Quality Concerns

If you believe there is a quality concern, message us directly with clear supporting images or video.

We take product concerns seriously and will review them properly.

---

## Wrong Item or Order Issue

If you receive the wrong item or there is a problem with order accuracy, contact us as soon as possible so we can review and help resolve it.

---

## Non-Return Situations

Products may not be eligible for return or exchange if they have been:
- heavily used
- altered
- customized
- damaged after delivery
- handled in a way that affects resale or reviewability

---

## Resolution Approach

When an issue is raised, we aim to:
- review it clearly
- respond professionally
- guide the customer on the most appropriate next step

---

## Need Help?

If you are unsure about a product before ordering, message us and we will help you choose carefully.

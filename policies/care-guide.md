# Care Guide

## How to Care for Your Hair

Good hair deserves good care.

Our premium raw Vietnamese hair is selected for softness, movement, fullness, and long-term value. Proper care helps maintain the look, feel, and lifespan of the hair.

---

## Core Care Principles

### 1. Be Gentle
Handle the hair with care, especially during washing, detangling, and styling.

### 2. Avoid Heavy Buildup
Too much product can weigh the hair down and affect softness and movement.

### 3. Keep It Clean
Regular cleaning helps maintain the finish and overall quality.

### 4. Use Heat Carefully
Too much heat can reduce softness over time.

### 5. Store It Properly
If the hair is not being worn, store it neatly to help maintain shape and condition.

---

## Washing Tips

- use gentle cleansing products
- avoid harsh treatment
- wash carefully, not aggressively
- rinse thoroughly
- allow the hair to dry properly before styling or storing

---

## Detangling Tips

- detangle gently
- start from the ends and work upward
- do not pull roughly
- be especially patient with textured options

---

## Styling Tips

- use heat carefully
- avoid unnecessary overstyling
- choose styling methods that help preserve softness and movement
- style according to the texture you purchased

---

## For Straight Hair

- keep product use light
- avoid heavy oils that make the hair limp
- wrap or store properly to help maintain a smooth finish

---

## For Wavy and Curly Hair

- handle curl patterns gently
- avoid rough brushing
- use methods that help the pattern stay defined
- be patient during detangling

---

## Closure and Frontal Care

- handle lace areas carefully
- avoid rough treatment around the base
- keep the finish neat and clean
- use care when customizing or styling

---

## Wig Care

- store wigs properly when not in use
- avoid crushing the shape
- handle the hairline and lace carefully
- clean and maintain according to wear frequency

---

## Everyday Maintenance

To keep your hair looking polished:
- keep it clean
- keep it detangled
- avoid excessive heat
- avoid product overload
- store it properly after wear if needed

---

## Before Reinstalling or Reusing

Before a reinstall or reuse:
- make sure the hair is clean
- detangle it properly
- inspect the lace or construction if applicable
- restyle gently as needed

# Paperclip AI Org Chart: Luxury Raw Direct

This document defines the functional departments and communication flows for the 17 AI Agents.

---

## 🏗️ Department 1: Management (The Strategy Core)
*Goal: Coordination, prioritization, and execution management.*

- **AI General Manager (GM):** High-level priorities and risk assessment.
- **AI Chief of Staff:** Milestone tracking and task breakdown.
- **AI Operations Coordinator:** SOP creation and workflow management.

---

## 📈 Department 2: Growth (Sales & Marketing)
*Goal: Customer acquisition, conversion, and brand voice.*

- **AI Sales Director:** Scripts, objection handling, and conversion logic.
- **AI Social Strategist:** Content calendars and campaign planning.
- **AI Brand Director:** Protecting the "Luxury Direct" voice and positioning.
- **AI Content Studio Lead:** Production of posts, blogs, and marketing copy.
- **AI Offer Architect:** Designing high-AOV bundles and promotions.

---

## 📦 Department 3: Product & Catalog (The Backbone)
*Goal: Data integrity, classification, and merchandising.*

- **AI Catalog Manager:** Media classification and asset mapping.
- **AI Merchandising Manager:** Grouping products into collections and tiers.
- **AI Product Copy Manager:** Writing high-conversion product pages.
- **AI Data Structuring Assistant:** Data normalization and cleanup.

---

## 🤝 Department 4: Customer Experience (Success & Retention)
*Goal: Guided buying, support, and after-purchase trust.*

- **AI Consultation Assistant:** Guided product recommendations.
- **AI Customer Service Lead:** Resolving issues and status inquiries.
- **AI Aftercare Writer:** Care guides and retention messaging.
- **AI Analytics Lead:** Measuring performance and reporting.
- **AI Documentation Clerk:** Repo-ready standardization of outputs.

---

## 🔄 The Logic Chain: How They Work Together

1. **New Raw Assets Arrive** -> *Catalog Manager* classifies them -> *Merchandising Manager* groups them into a "Body Wave Collection" -> *Product Copy Manager* writes the page.
2. **Launch Campaign Needed** -> *Social Strategist* builds the 30-day plan -> *Content Studio Lead* writes the 30 captions -> *Brand Director* reviews for "Luxury Voice."
3. **WhatsApp Inquiry** -> *Consultation Assistant* recommends a set -> *Sales Director* handles price objections -> *Offer Architect* proposes a "Bundle & Save" deal.

---

## 🛠️ Operating Instructions for the Founder
When initiating a task, mention the **Department** or **Agent Name**.

*Example: "Growth Dept, we need a 7-day 'Deep Wave' focused campaign for Instagram. Social Strategist, build the calendar; Content Lead, write the captions."*
